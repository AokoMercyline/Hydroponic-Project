Slanting style

  • When the water runs to one trough and full it will automatically flow through the other troughs.
  • After the water as go into all the troughs, the water goes into a lower tank which pump the water again to the initial tank. This process will be automatically, that is why it is called counter current water flow because it is a two way channel.
  • The troughs are arranged in a slanting position or horizontally to allow the water to flow from one trough to the other trough and so one and so forth.
